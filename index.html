Founded on the belief that coffee is more than a drink—it's a tactile connection to the earth and the hands that harvest it.

Our Craft

The Roastery

Our small-batch process is an exercise in restraint. We roast in a vintage 1968 iron drum, allowing us to feel the heat and hear the "first crack" with visceral precision. Every batch is limited to twelve kilograms to ensure absolute consistency.

Sourcing Philosophy

Direct Trade, Real Impact

We bypass conventional distribution chains to work directly with coffee producers. By visiting estates in Sidama and Santander, we build deep relationships, negotiate prices above fair trade standards, and help fund local farm infrastructure.
